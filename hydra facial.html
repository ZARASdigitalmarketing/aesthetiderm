Hydrafacial

What is a HydraFacial?


According to board-certified cosmetic dermatologist Dr. Ariel Ostad, “The HydraFacial is the only hydra-dermabrasion procedure that uses patented technology to cleanse, extract, and hydrate.” And yes, HydraFacial is a branded experience. Spas have to be registered and licensed to market themselves as offering HydraFacials.


What do HydraFacials do?


For those who wear makeup a lot or simply want a deeper clean than usual, HydraFacials are a great way to cleanse the skin. According to board-certified dermatologist Dr. Anthony Rossi Jr., “facials such as HydraFacials are a way to remove the buildup of sebum and debris that cover the top of our skin. It helps to loosen and unclog pores, and combines an exfoliation process of removing the top layer of dead skin cells, the stratum corneum, with hydration and cleansing of water.”


How is it Done?


During a HydraFacial session at our clinic, your skin is thoroughly treated through a carefully curated process. We begin by exfoliating and cleansing your skin, efficiently extracting impurities using a specially designed, patented device. This not only purifies your skin but also delivers deep hydration. This process is boosted by a vortex swirling action that simultaneously removes dead skin cells, dirt, and debris, leaving your skin refreshed and revitalized. It’s a comprehensive skin rejuvenation system designed to nourish, cleanse, and soothe your skin from within.


Benefits of Hydrafacial:


1. Compatible with all skin types. HydraFacial can be used by anyone, including those with sensitive skin, acne, dry and oily skin.
2. A consistent outcome. With HydraFacial technology controlling much of the treatment, results are consistent and high-quality.
3. There is no downtime. HydraFacials also have no downtime, which is another reason people love them. It is also possible to apply makeup the same day, though most people prefer not to mask the glow from this treatment.
4. Achieve results immediately. Results are immediate due to the noninvasive nature of the treatment. To achieve a healthy, luminous glow, it is recommended before weddings and events.


PRE-PROCEDURE CARE:


1. For at least 48 hours before your treatment, do not wax or use depilatory products.
2. You should discontinue using generic tretinoin or topical Retin-A at least 48 hours before your treatment.
3. After a facial treatment, do not shave your face.


POST-PROCEDURE CARE:


1. For at least 48 hours after treatment, avoid over-the-counter acne medications, such as benzoyl peroxide and salicylic acid.
2. For at least 24 hours after treatment, do not expose yourself to intense heat.
3. Makeup should not be applied until the day after your treatment.
