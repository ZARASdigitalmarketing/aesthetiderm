Whitening Injection

What are skin whitening injections?


Skin whitening injections are intravenous infusions of glutathione and vitamin C compounds scientifically proven to reduce pigmentation and enhance the skin’s natural glow.


Are skin whitening injections safe?


Skin whitening injections are safe when administered by a medical professional. However, caution should be exercised in individuals with chronic kidney failure, liver problems, and asthma. A medical professional should closely monitor the use of this drug.


How do skin whitening injections work?


Skin whitening injections work by reducing wrinkles and fine lines, greatly increasing the effect of whitening on the whole body, reducing facial pores and improving skin complexion, reducing dark spot areas due to sun exposure, upholding the production of joints and cartilage, making the skin glowing and pinkish, maintaining an attractive complexion and acne-free, helping to tighten the skin, and improving skin roughness.
