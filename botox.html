Botox

In the world, Botox is the most frequently performed procedure for treating facial wrinkles. It is considered to be a safe, efficient, and effective way to treat fine lines and wrinkles around the eyes. Botox can also be used on the forehead between the eyes, Dr. Marya Hamid at the Aesthetics Clinic provides the best Botox treatment in Kohat , Pakistan.


What areas of the body can be treated with Botox?


Cosmetically, the injectable can be used in the following areas:
1. the area between the eyebrows to treat moderate to severe frown lines
2. around the eyes, commonly known as crow’s feet lines


How Does Botox Work?


Botox is a muscle stimulant that blocks nerve impulses to muscles, which relaxes and softens wrinkles. It is usually used to treat forehead lines, lines around the eyes, and frown lines. Botox is injected with a small needle into specific muscles with only minor discomfort. It generally takes 7 to 14 days for the results to show. For precautions, do not rub the injection site for 24 hours afterward to prevent it from spreading.


How much time does the procedure take?


The entire procedure takes approximately 10 minutes.


What are the benefits of Botox?


Treatments offer an array of benefits, including these three:


1. Smooth out forehead lines


Botox works on both forehead lines and brow creases, which can make your skin look smoother, younger, and more refreshed.


2. Prevent fine lines and wrinkles


Having Botox injections early, in your mid to late 20s or early 30s, for instance — could help prevent early signs of aging by reducing the muscle signaling that leads to lines and folds in the skin. And by combining Botox with fillers, you could help maintain your youthful-looking skin.


3. Make you look less stressed


Lines and wrinkles may not only make you look older, but they may make you look more stressed out, too. By smoothing out these lines, Botox can help you look more relaxed and rested.
