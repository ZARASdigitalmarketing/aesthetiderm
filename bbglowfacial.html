BB Glow Facial

What is the BB Glow Facial?


The BB Glow treatment is the latest and fastest-growing treatment in Europe and Asia. Now, it’s making an appearance in the United States. This treatment is a 100% natural, semi-permanent foundation for your skin. It will even out imperfections and discoloration, leaving your skin looking like you’re wearing the highest quality, lightweight face makeup.
The BB Glow serum is a scientifically proven and tested skin care product that provides coverage for facial imperfections including hyperpigmentation, redness, dark circles, and large pores. The premium BB Glow ampoule serum with whitening and skin rejuvenating properties will help alleviate major skin problems without overstimulating the skin. The color component of the BB glow formula brightens the skin and gives it a soft, glossy look with ingredients including niacinamide and peptides. This will give you a natural-looking, waterproof base that will NOT rub off!


The Benefits Of BB Glow Facial


1. Instantly glowing and radiant complexion
2. Hydrated skin
3. Reduction of fine lines and wrinkles
4. Reduction of dark under-eye circles
5. Reduction of blackheads and pores
6. Infusion of antioxidants, vitamins, and anti-aging ingredients
7. Evens out blemishes and discoloration
8. Reduces melanin content in the skin before and after UV exposure
9. Covers up redness (rosacea) and broken capillaries


Who are good candidates for the BB Glow facial treatment?


Good candidates include individuals with dull and uneven skin texture, freckles, hyperpigmentation, acne scars, age spots, sun spots, early aging, and lax skin, and much more! If you’re looking to instantly unblock and minimize pores, hydrate the skin, reduce and improve scarring, and reduce inflammation, the BB Glow facial may be the right treatment for you.


BB Glow Facial Post Care


There’s little to no downtime after treatment. However, clients should avoid hot baths, saunas, and any strenuous exercise. Sun exposure should be avoided for the first couple of days after treatment. Don’t use harsh soaps or scrubs or apply makeup within 24 hours. It’s also required to wear SPF daily after treatment.


Contradictions


The BB Glow Facial should be avoided by those who:
1. Are pregnant and/or nursing
2. Have skin cancer, skin disease, active infections, or open wounds
3. Are taking Accutane
4. Have allergies to any ingredients being used
5. Have had injectable fillers applied within 2 weeks of receiving the treatment
6. Are on blood thinners


How Long Does BB Glow Treatment Last?


With the right number of treatments and proper aftercare, BB glow treatment can last up to half a year. Unlike many other beauty industry products that have minimum requirements for results or require constant maintenance services like touch-ups; this type is cumulative so with each package you buy your desired look will be maintained longer while requiring fewer sessions at once!


Does BB Glow Treatment Hurt?


Unlike other procedures, BB glow is not painful. You do not need time to recover as you are good to carry on with your daily routine right after the treatment. This is because of the advanced technology used for this treatment. It uses microneedles that can barely be felt and can offer instant results without the pain.
